About us

Authentic Vegan and
Gluten-free Desserts

Faerie Food was started in early 2020 in Columbia, SC. Due to the lack of plant based and gluten free options in the area, we decided to set up shop!

Using only all natural and healthy ingredients we create delicious desserts for the health conscious without sacrificing great taste.
